Global Projects

Partnering with nonprofits across 25+ countries to measure and improve water and food security outcomes

Over the last decade, I have led more than 100 evaluations of international development programs across Sub-Saharan Africa, Asia, the Caribbean, and Middle East. This work has supported governmental and non-governmental organizations around the world, including USAID, The Millennium Challenge Corporation, The International Rescue Committee, BRAC, World Vision, Catholic Relief Services, Heifer International, Helvetas, Welthungerhilfe, Water for People, and charity: water, among many others.

Although my experience is largely in food and water security, I have evaluated projects focused on conflict mitigation, biodiversity conservation, climate resilience, tourism development, teacher training, and digital technology applications for development.

Featured Partnership: charity: water

Director of Project Evaluations

As Director of Project Evaluations at charity: water, I oversee the evaluation and monitoring of water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH) programs across 22 countries. Our work helps ensure that communities receive sustainable access to clean water through evidence-based program design and rigorous impact measurement.

charity: water has funded over 171,000 water projects across 29 countries, bringing clean water to more than 20 million people worldwide. Through innovative sensor technology and community-led monitoring, we ensure these projects remain functional and continue serving communities for years to come.
